How to Point Your Domain
to Your New Website

A quick guide for wolfiescafemanly.com

Your domain is registered through Squarespace Domains (formerly Google Domains). To connect it to your new website, you just need to update the nameservers. Here's how:

1

Go to domains.squarespace.com

Log in with the Google account you used for Google Domains.

2

Click on wolfiescafemanly.com

You'll see it listed on your dashboard.

3

Click "DNS" or "DNS Settings" in the sidebar

This is where your domain's settings live.

4

Find the "Nameservers" section

Scroll down until you see nameserver settings.

5

Click "Edit" or "Use Custom Nameservers"

Switch from the default nameservers to custom ones.

6

Remove the old nameservers

Delete or replace all four of these:

ns-cloud-d1.googledomains.com
ns-cloud-d2.googledomains.com
ns-cloud-d3.googledomains.com
ns-cloud-d4.googledomains.com
7

Add the new Cloudflare nameservers

Type in these two nameservers (Zac will provide the exact ones):

_____________________.ns.cloudflare.com
_____________________.ns.cloudflare.com

Zac will text or write these in for you before you start.

8

Click "Save"

That's it! Changes can take up to 24 hours to take effect, but usually happen within a few minutes.

Will this affect my email?

No. If you're using Google Workspace (Gmail) for your email, your MX records are separate and will be re-added on the new nameservers. Your email will keep working as normal. If you have any concerns at all, don't worry — Zac will handle everything to make sure nothing breaks.
